How to Use Actions

What are Actions?

Actions let you send quick messages with just a tap. Instead of typing, simply tap an action button to send pre-written responses, reactions, or custom messages. Perfect for when you're on the go or need to reply quickly.

Create your custom action with these steps:

  1. Set Action Name

    Give your action a clear, memorable title that describes what it does. This name is crucial because Siri will use it to recognize your voice commands, and it will appear in the Shortcuts app. Choose something easy to say and remember, like "Tell Mom I'm coming" or "Send location to work."

  2. Select Action Color

    Pick a color to personalize your action. This will help you quickly identify your actions visually.

  3. Choose Contact

    Select the person you want to message from your Sparq contacts. All your contacts have already been imported or added manually to the app, so simply browse through the list to find the right person.

  4. Pick Messaging Service

    Select which platform you want to use for sending the message. Currently available options include iMessage, WhatsApp, Telegram, and Viber, with more messaging services being added in future updates.

  5. Select Contact Number

    If your chosen contact has multiple phone numbers, pick the specific number associated with their account on your selected messaging service.

  6. Choose Your Message

    Pick from your pre-written messages that are already saved in Sparq. Simply select the message that fits your needs, whether it's a quick response, greeting, or status update. You can also choose messages that include your current location if you want to share where you are (pro).

Make sure to press the "Save" button to store your action and make it ready for use.
Once set up, you can run this action with a single tap from your phone or watch, or by asking Siri using the name you chose in step 1.

Now, just select your choices to complete the action setup!
